Explore wellspring of the Hebrew text...don't just drink downstream!

The subtle nuances in meaning we derive from reading our modern English translations of the Greek New Testament, while edifying, are not always entirely accurate (in the finer details). Through exploring the Hebrew texts it becomes clear that idiomatic Hebrew expressions and cultural idioms are oftentimes overlooked in the Greek text, as are grammatical nuances, such as those do with the 'gender' of the word Spirit in the third person (she). Here, the Hebrew text in the Du Tillet text (Heb. 132) appears with the major Shem Tov variants in braces.
