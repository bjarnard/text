And after six days Yeshua took Kepha, and Ya'aqov, and Yochanan his borther, and took them up upon a high mountain by themselves. And his face was transformed before them, and his face shone like [the] sun. And his clothes were white like snow. And behold, Moshe and EliYahu appeared to them, speaking with him {and telling Yeshua everything that would meet him in Yerushalayim. And Peter and his companions were sleeping. Asleep but not asleep. Awake but not awake}. And Kepha answered, and he said to Yeshua, "Adonai, it's good for us to be here. If you want, then we will make three tents {dwellings}: one for you, one for Moshe, one for EliYahu {Eloah}" {since he did not know what he was saying}. They were yet talking, and behold, a splendorous cloud covered them. And behold, a voice spoke from the cloud: "This one, he is my beloved Son who I have been pleased with. {My precious Son, and my delight [is] in him}. Listen to him."
