These are the generations of Yeshua son of David son of Avraham - Avraham begot Yitskhaq - and Yitskhaq begot Yaakov - Yaacov begot Yehudah and his brothers - Yehudah begot Peretz and Zerach from Tamar - Peretz begot Hetsron - Hetsron begat Ram - and Ram begat Aminadav - Aminadav begat Nachshon - Nachshon begot Salmon - Salmon begot Boaz of Rehab - Boaz begot Oved of Ruth - and Oved begot Yishai - Yishai begat David the King, David begat Shlomo from the wife of Uriah - Shlomo begot Rechavam - Rechavam begat Aviyah - Aviyah begot Asa - Asa begot Yehoshaphat - Yehoshaphat begat Yoram - Yoram begot Uzziyah - Uzziyah begot Yotam - Yotam begot Akhaz - Akhaz begot Hezekiah - Hezekiah begat Manasseh - Manasseh begot Amon - Amon begat Yoshiyah - Yoshiyah begot Yekoniyah and his brothers in the Babylonian exile - Yekoniah begot Shaltiel - Shaltiel begot Zerubbabel - Zerubbabel begot Avihud - and Avihud begot Abner - Abner begot Eliakim - Elyakim begot Azur, Azur begot Tsadoq, Tsadoq begot Amon, Amon begot Elihud, Elihud begot Elazar, Elazar begot Matan, Matan begot Yaakov, Yaakov begat Yoseph the husband {father} of Miryam, from whom Yeshua was begotten, who is called Messiah. And all the generations from Abraham until David [are] four and ten generations, and from the Babylonian exile until the Messiah [are] four and ten generations. And the generation of Yeshua the Messiah was thus: from after his mother Miryam was betrothed to Yoseph, before he went to her she was found pregnant by the Set-apart Spirit, and Yoseph her husband was a righteous man {and he was not willing to dwell with her} and he did not want to turn her in {bind her over} to be put to death, nor to reveal her {to bring her to shame}, only this was in his heart: to leave her in secret {to cover her} and as he was thinking of this the Angel appeared to him in a dream saying ‘Joseph ben David, fear not to take Miryam [as] your wife because what will be born of her, from the Holy Spirit [is] he, for of the Set-apart Spirit she is pregnant, and behold, she will beget a son, and you shall declare his name Yeshua, for he shall save his people from all their sins, and all this is to fill up what was said by Yehovah at the hand of the prophet Yeshiyah saying, ‘Behold, the maiden shall conceive, and shall give birth to a son, and his name shall be called Emmanuel.’ And Yoseph awoke from his sleep and he did according to what the Angel of Yehovah commanded him, and he took her to wife and he did not know her until she had given birth to her firstborn son, and he called his name Yeshua.
