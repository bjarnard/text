Then the Kingdom of Heaven is like unto to twelve virgins that took their cresset torches, and went forth to meet the bridegroom. Five of them were foolish and five of them were wise. The foolish ones, when they took their cresset torches, did not take oil with them. And the wise ones all took oil with them with the cresset torches, and when the bridegroom was late, they all napped and slumbered. And in the middle of the night, there was a cry, and behold, the bridegroom came, and they went forth to meet him, as did all these virgins, and preparing their cresset torches. And the foolish ones said to the wise ones, 'give us of yoThereur oil' for our cresset torches have been extinguished. And the wise ones answered, and said to them, 'It is not given to ye, lest we lack.' And ye, go to them who sell to procure, and at that, for yourselves. And in their going to procure, the bridegroom came. Those prepared came with him to the wedding, and the entrance was closed, and afterwards the remnant of the other virgins came, saying, 'lord, lord, open to us', and he answered, saying, 'Confirmed [is] what I am saying to ye, that I have not known ye.' And therefore be wakeful, for ye do no know the day or the hour given that he, the Son of man, will come. For [it is] as a man that goes to sojourn in a province of the sea, and calls to his servant and gives them his possessions. To one he gave five kekars, and to one, two, and to one, one; to every man gave he according to his power, and he departed right away. And [the one] who took the five kekars went and traded with them, and he procured five others. And [the one] who took procured another two more, and [the one] who took the one, went, and dug in earth, and hid it in earth. And it was after much time, his lord returned, and he made an account with them. And the one who took the five kekars drew close, and he brought near yet five other kekars, saying, 'lord, you gave me five kekars, and behold I've added to them five others more.' And his lord said to him, 'brother, because of being a good servant, and faithful in the little, come, and I will appoint you over the much. Go, enter into the joy of your lord. And he also drew near who took the two kekars, and he said, 'my lord, you have given me two kekars, and behold yet these two more I have procured.' And his lord said to him, 'brother, sinced you were a good servant, and faithful over the little, please, and I will appoint you over the much. Go, enter into the joy of your lord.' And he drew near who took the one kekar, and he said, 'my lord, I know that you are a harsh man, and you reap where you did not sow, and you harvest where you did not scatter [seed]. And I saw, and I went, and I hid your kekar in earth. Behold, what is yours [is] yours. ‫:‬
