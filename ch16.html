And he answered them, and he said to them, "when it becomes evening, ye say, 'the sky will be splendorous in the future, for the sky is red.' And in the light of the day, 'T here will be a storm, for the sky is overcast and red.' Behold, ye know [how] to judge the face of the sky, and the signs of the times ye are not able to see. An evil and scheming lineage seeks a sign, and a sign will not be given it, except the sign of Jonah the prophet. And he left them, and walked. And when his taught ones came across the sea, they forgot to take bread. And he said to them, "Look, and gaurd yourselves against that which is leavened by Prushim and Zadoqim." And they reasoned amonst themselves, saying, "Because we didn't take bread?" And Yeshua knew, and he said, "What [are] ye thinking, small-ones [in] faith, that ye have not taken bread? Do ye yet understand? Do you not remember the five loaves of bread for the five thousand men, and how many baskets ye took up? And were not four thousand men satisfied with bread? And how many baskets did ye take up? And how do you not understand that I spoke not of bread to ye, 'Keep yourselves from the food leavened by the Prushim and the Zadoqim?'" Then they heard and understood that he spoke not of keeping oneself of leavened bread, but from the teaching of the Prushim and Zadoqim.
