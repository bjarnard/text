And Prushim and Zadoqim came near to him to test him, and they asked him that they might see one sign from the heavens. And he answered them, and he said to them, "when it becomes evening, ye say, 'the sky will be splendorous in the future, for the sky is red.' And in the light of the day, 'There will be a storm, for the sky is overcast and red.' Behold, ye know [how] to judge the face of the sky, and the signs of the times ye are not able to see. An evil and scheming lineage seeks a sign, and a sign will not be given it, except the sign of Jonah the prophet. And he left them, and walked. And when his taught ones came across the sea, they forgot to take bread. And he said to them, "Look, and gaurd yourselves against that which is leavened by Prushim and Zadoqim." And they reasoned amonst themselves, saying, "Because we didn't take bread?" And Yeshua knew, and he said, "What [are] ye thinking, small-ones [in] faith, that ye have not taken bread? Do ye yet understand? Do you not remember the five loaves of bread for the five thousand men, and how many baskets ye took up? And were not four thousand men satisfied with bread? And how many baskets did ye take up? And how do you not understand that I spoke not of bread to ye, 'Keep yourselves from the food leavened by the Prushim and the Zadoqim?'" Then they heard and understood that he spoke not of keeping oneself of leavened bread, but from the teaching of the Prushim and Zadoqim. And Yeshua came to the borders of Cesaerea Phillipos, and he lifted up [a question] to one and all, saying, "who do the children of mankind say the Son of Man is?" And they said, "some Yochanan the Immerser, and some EliYah, and others YirmeYahu or one of the prophets." And Shimon Kepha answered, saying, "You're him. Messiah, Son of the living Elohim." And Yeshua answered, and he said to him, "happy are you Shimon son of Yonah, for flesh and blood did not reveal this, but it was expressely revealed to you by my Father that is in Heaven. And I say to you, that you are Kepha [stone] {Evan [rock]} and upon this stone I will build [Evoneh] my assembly, and the gates of the underworld shall not prevail against you, and to you I will give the keys of the Kingdom of Heaven, and all that you bind on earth will be bound in Heaven, and all that you specify on earth will be allowed in Heaven. Then he commanded his taught-ones that they tell no man that he [was] Yeshua the Messiah. Then he began to make it known to his taught-ones that it was necessary to go to Yerushalayim, and to bear great torment and blasphemies there from the elders and scribes, and from the chief priests, and to be killed, and to rise on the third day. And Kepha took him, and started to correct him, saying, "[God] forbid you, Adonai. All this shall not happen to you." And he turned himself, and said to Kepha, "Follow me, Satan. You are a stumbling block to me, for you do not taste those those [things] that are of Elohim, but those that are of men." Then Yeshua said to his taught-ones, "whosoever wants to follow me, let him reject himself, and lift up his execution stake, and follow me. For whosoever delights in saving his soul, he will lose it, and whosoever loses his soul for my sake, he will find it. For what profit [is there] for a man if he procures all the world and carries injury in his soul? Or what exchange will the man give for his soul? For the Son of man will come in the glory of his Father with his angels, and then he will make a recompense to all, man [by] man, according to his deeds. Confirmed is what I say to ye, there are those standing here that will not taste death until they see the Son of man entering his Kingdom.
