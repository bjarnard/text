And Prushim and Zadoqim came near to him to test him, and they asked him that they might see one sign from the heavens. And he answered them, and he said to them, "when it becomes evening, ye say, 'the sky will be splendorous in the future, for the sky is red. And in the light of the day, there will be a storm, for the sky is overcast and red.' Behold, ye know the right-ruling of the sky
