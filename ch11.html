Hebrew Matthew Ch.11

And Yeshua cried out [the names of] twelve of his taught-ones, and he gave to them {power over all unclean spirits} authority over unclean spirits in order to remove them and to heal all sickness {and legion} and pain. And these [are] the names of they [who were] the ten {12} and two sent-ones: The first, Shimon called Kefa, and Andriyah his brother. Philipos and Bartulomiyahu. Ya’aqov [of] Zabdi, and Yochanan his brother. Tumaso, and Matteyhu {Mattityahu} who was a master transgressor/highway [toll] master. And Ya’aqov [of] Alphi and Todiyahu. Shimon the Canaanite and Yehudah Iscaryoutu that bound him over to death. And Yeshua sent these twelve and he commanded them, saying, “Do not walk upon the highway of the nations, and to the cities of the Samaritans do not enter, but go to the wasted flock within [the borders of] the house of Israel, and proclaim, saying, ‘Repent! Repent! For the Kingdom of Heaven is near to come.’ Heal the sick, raise up the dead, cleanse those with skin afflictions, and remove the demons. Without [cost] ye have taken, and without [price] shall ye give. Do not procure gold and silver, and no [taking] coins in your waist-pouch. Don’t glean in on the way. Don’t [have] two coats and no mantles and no bed, for the worker is worthy of his food, and in whatsoever city or hamlet ye enter, ask who there is honorable, and there ye shall dwell until ye go forth from thither. And when ye are brought into a house, ask after its peace, saying, ‘Shalom to this house.’ And if this house will be honorable, your peace/greeting shall return to ye. And the man that does not take ye, and does not hear the voice of your words, go forth outside the house or from the city and cast forth also the dust from your feet {remove your feet from the dust}. Confirmed [is what] I say to ye, it will be easier for the land of Sodom and Gemorah on the judgment day, more so than for that city. Behold, ye are sent forth like a flock into the midst of wolves, therefore be ye shrewd as snakes, lest they bind ye over to the to assemblies or in the congregations, and they chastise ye with whips, and lead ye [with faces] covered to officers and kings to afflict [ye] in their assemblies and before the nations. And when they bind ye, don’t think about how or what to speak, for he will give ye what, in in your mouths, you shall speak in that hour, what you shall answer; it’s not ye who speaks, but rather your Father’s Spirit—she speaks [through] ye. And brother shall bind brother over to be put to death, and [the] father [the] son, and the sons shall rise against the fathers to have them executed, and ye shall be hated by all men because of my name. But he who who stands [firm] until the end, he shall be saved. And when they chase ye from one city, flee ye to another. Confirmed [is what] I am saying to ye: ye shall not finish the cities of Israel until the Son of Man comes. The taught-one is not greater than his teacher. And a servant [is] not above his lord, but it is enough for the taught-one to be like his Rabbi, or for the servant [to be] like his lord. If they call the master of the house the master of flies [Baal Zebub], how much more so the sons of the house. So don’t fear them, for there is no covered matter than shall not be revealed, or secret that won’t be made known. What I speak to ye in darkness, say it in [the] light. And what ye hear in the ear, proclaim it upon the roof tops. And do not fear them that kill the body, but the soul they cannot slay. Should ye not fear him who can end the soul and the body in the destruction of Gehinnom? Are not two birds sold for a small coin? And not one of them falls to the ground without your Father. So are all the hairs on your head counted, therefore do not fear, for ye [are] goodly [people] like many birds.
