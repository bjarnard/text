An on the evening of Shabbat, the oneness of Shabbat was illuminated. Miriam Magdalit, and other Miriams came to see the tomb, and behold, there was a great shaking beause the Angel of Yehovah came down from the Skies and approached, and rolled the stone away from the opening, and sat upon it. And his appearance was like lightning, and his clothes like snow, and the gaurds were troubled by his awesomeness, and they were like dead ones. And the Angel answeredd, and he said to [the] women, "do not fear. Know ye that Yeshua ye seek is not here, for he has risen as he said. Come and see the place in which my lord lay." And at once, go ye and tell his taught-ones that he rose from the dead. And behold, he goes before ye into Galil, and there ye shall see him, and behold, I have told you. And in a hurry they went out of the tomb, for they were afraid, and had great joy! And they ran to tell his taught ones. And as they went to tell his taught-ones, behold, Yeshua came to call to them, saying, "Shalom to ye." And behold they drew near and took his feet, and bowed to him. Then Yeshua said to them, "Do not fear, go ye, and tell my brother that I go to Galil, for there they shall see me." And so they went. And behold, some of the gaurds went to they city, and they told head of the priests all that happened, and they elders, and they took counsel. And they gave much silver to the soldiers, saying, "say ye that his taught-ones came at night and stole him while we lay down. And if the official hears this, we will persaude him, and we will secure ye. And the took the silver, and they did as they taught. And the saying was so published by the Jews until this day. And the eleven taught-ones went to Galil to the mountain where Yeshua bore witness to them, and he spoke to them, saying, "all authority is givven to me in heaven and on earth. Go ye! Go and teach all people groups and immerse them in the Name of the Father and the Son [of the] Holy Spirit, and teach them to gaurd all that I have commanded ye, and behold I am with ye all the days until the end of the world.
