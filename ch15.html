Then Scribes and Prushim from Yerushalayim drew near to him, saying, "why [do] your taught-ones transgress the decrees of the elders? For they wash not their hands when they eat bread. And he answered them, and he said, "and why do you trangress the commandments of Elohim for the sake of your decrees? Is it not written in your Torah, from the mouth of Elohim, 'honor your father and mother' and [it is also] written 'he who curses his father and mother shall surely die?' and ye say, 'all who say to father and to mother, 'every present from me, this is your benefit', so he does not [have to] honor his father and mother.' And ye pit the commandments of Elohim because of your decrees. You hypocrites! How exceedingly well YeshiYahu did prophecy about ye, saying, "this nation honors me with its mouth and with its lips, but its heart is far from me. And their fear towards me is taught [by] commands of men.
