And great crowds drew near to him, and there were mute people, blind people, lame people, disabled people, and many other people, and they set them before his feet, and he healed them. And his taught-ones said to him, "we don't have enough bread in the desert to satisfy this people." And Yeshua said to them, "how many loaves of bread do you have? And they answered and said, "Seven. And some small fish." And he commanded the people to sit upon the ground. And he took the seven loaves of bread and the fish, and gave thanks, and broke [them], and gave to his taught-ones, and they gave to the people, and all of them ate, and were satisfied. And from what was left over from the crowds, they lifted up seven baskets full. And they that ate were four-thousand men, not including children and women. And the crowds left, and he went ascending into a boat, and they went to the borders of Magdala.
