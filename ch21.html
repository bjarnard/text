And when they drew near to Yerushalaim, and came to Beit-Phagei, to the moutain of the Olives, then Yeshua sent two taught-ones, and said to them, "go to the villa that is before ye, and right away ye shall find there a bound donkey, and a colt with here. Loose [them] and bring [them] near to me. And if a man says, "What?" to ye, say ye, "because Adonai has need of them." And right away he will let ye. And this is to establish what is said at the hand of the prophet, saying, "Say to [the] daughter of Tsion, 'behold, your king comes to you meek, and riding upon a donkey, and upon a colt, a son of a she-ass." And the taught-ones went, and they did as Yeshua commanded them. And they brought near the she-ass and the colt, and they placed their garments upon them.
