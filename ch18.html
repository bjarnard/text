In that hour, the taught-ones came near to Yeshua, saying, "who will be great in the Kingdom of Heaven?" And Yeshua took one young lad, and stood him among them, and he said, "confirmed [is what] I say to ye, if ye don't repent, and become like young lads, you will not enter the Kingdom of Heaven. And all who humble themselves like a this young lad, he will be great in the Kingdom of Heaven. And whosoever recieves a young lad like this, in my name, he recieves me. But whosoever makes one of these small-ones stumble, that believe me, [it would be] good for him that he have an upper millstone hung around his neck to cast him into the depths of the sea. Woe to the world because of offenses. It's necessary that offenses come, but woe to the man from whom offense comes. And if your hand or your foot offend you, cut it off, and cast it from you, for [it would be] good for you to enter into life weak or lame, then to be sent with your hands and feet to the eternal fire. And if your eye makes you stumble, pluck it out, and cast it from you, for [it would be] better to enter with one eye into life, than being sent with two eyes to fire of Gehinnom. Seethat you don't despise one of these little-ones, for I say to ye, that their angels in Heaven always behold the face of my Father in Heaven, for the Son of man [comes] to save what was lost.
