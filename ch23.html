[about]. But don't do according to their case law, for they will speak [the decree] but will not do [them as a matter of halakhot] [thus invalidating them]. And they bind heavy burdens without carrying them! And they place them upon the shoulders of men, and they won't move them by themselves. And also, all their works they do in order to seen by sons of men. For they make wide their totafot (frontlets), and make the wings of their mantles big. And they they love the chief seats in feasts of the evening, and the chief seats in the houses of assembly, premiums in the market, and to be proclaimed Rabbi by men. And ye, don't proclaim "Rabbi", for one is your Rabbi, and he is the Messiah. For all of you are siblings. Therefore don't call yourselves 'father' upon the earth, for one is your Father, that is [He who is] in Heaven. And be not called teachers, for One, he is your Teacher, and He is the Messiah. He who [is] great of you, he shall be your minister. And whosoever is exhalts one's self, he will be be made low, and whosoever that will become lowly, he will be exalted. Woe to ye, scribes and Prushim, hypocrites, that shut up the Kingdom of Heaven before the faces of the sons of men, for you entered not, and did not allow those willing-ones to go. Woe to ye scribes and Prushim, hypocrites, because you eat up the house of the widow. For this reason you make drawn-out prayers! Therefore you shall receive drawn out judgment.
