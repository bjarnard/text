At that time Horodos, one of four princes, heard proclamation of Yeshua, and he said to his young men {his servants}, this is he [who is called] Yochanan the Immerser. He has risen from the dead, and therefore the powers work through him {behold, I believe these are the miracles done by Yochanan the Immerser}. And Horodos had Yochanan seized, and bound him, and put him in the prison house before Horodosit the wife of his brother [Herod II] because she said to him, "John is not allowed to be with you" {because he was reproving him, that he not take Ordisa to wife, since she was wife to his brother} And he wanted to kill him, yet he feared the people, for he was a prophet in their eyes.
